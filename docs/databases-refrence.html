07 — Key Rules · Business Layer
Enforce these in the Business Layer — never in DAOs or Servlets
VIN is exactly 17 characters. Validate before any INSERT or UPDATE on Vehicles.
Sold is terminal. No status transitions out of Sold under any circumstance.
One active Reservation per vehicle. Check before inserting a new Reservation row.
Sale triggers status change. Recording a Sale must flip Vehicle.status to Sold atomically.
Customer must be complete before sale. first_name, last_name, and email all required.
Every vehicle change logs to VehicleChangeLog. INSERT, UPDATE, and DELETE all get a row.
Vehicle must be Available or Reserved to sell. Reject sale attempt if status is already Sold.
Reservation converts on sale. Set Reservation.status = Converted when the vehicle is sold.
